About SJ Stitches

SJ Stitches is the creative vision of Sarah Johnson, a passionate fashion designer dedicated to bringing elegance and personality to every garment. Based in Ughelli North, Delta State, we specialize in custom couture that reflects the vibrant spirit of Nigerian fashion while incorporating contemporary design elements. Our atelier is a space where your fashion dreams come to life through expert craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of style. Whether you're looking for traditional attire with a modern twist, corporate wear that commands respect, or show-stopping pieces for special occasions, Sarah and her team work closely with each client to ensure perfect fit, exceptional quality, and designs that exceed expectations.
